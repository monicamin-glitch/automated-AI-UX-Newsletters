## Rendering rules

- Do not display subcategory headings.
- Do not visually highlight UX AI Foundations; hierarchy comes from its full-width placement.
- Align each category icon vertically with its title.
- Render each link as a compact, equal-width row with an external-link icon.
- Keep the page focused on AI for UX, not general design guidance.
- Preserve the category and resource order defined here.
- On narrow screens, stack all three category cards and switch the Foundations links to one column.
